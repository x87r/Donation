About Us & Contact

About Helper Hands

Helper Hands is a non-profit initiative founded by Radhey with the mission to provide meals to those who need it the most - hungry children and stray dogs.

We believe that no child or animal should go to bed hungry. With your support, we can ensure that nutritious meals reach those who need them the most.

Every donation is tracked transparently, and donors receive photos and updates about the meals provided in their name.
