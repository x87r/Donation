About Helper Hands

Founded by Radhey, Helper Hands is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing meals to underprivileged children and stray dogs. Our motto "WE FOR YOU" reflects our commitment to serve those in need.

Every donation, no matter how small, makes a significant difference. We ensure transparency by sending pictures and updates to donors within 6-7 days of their contribution.
